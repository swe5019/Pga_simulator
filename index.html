Why SlateSims wins

In DFS golf, edge comes from leverage — being over or under the field on the right players. Leverage is only as good as the ownership number it's measured against. That number is what we obsess over.

Everything you need to attack a slate

🎲 Hole-by-hole simulations

Every golfer's tournament simulated thousands of times — birdie streaks, bogey-free rounds, eagles, the missed cut. Real boom/bust shape, not a flat projection with a guessed standard deviation.

🏆 Optimizer + Sim Score

Build hundreds of lineups ranked by a leverage-aware score that blends projection, ceiling, and ownership. Per-player min/max exposure, locks, and bans.

💰 Contest ROI

Score every lineup against an ownership-weighted field on the exact DraftKings payout table — expected ROI, cash %, and win % before you enter.

✋ Hand Build

For single-entry players: drop golfers in one at a time and watch your salary remaining update live. Save lineups and export straight to DK.

📈 Published accuracy

A live page tracks our ownership MAE vs. actual results, event over event. Proof, not promises — and your reason to trust the leverage.

⬆️ One-click DraftKings

Import salaries, then export an upload-ready CSV or fill your reserved entries template in DK's Name (ID) format. Names map to upload IDs automatically.

FAQ

Is this just another optimizer?

No. The optimizer is table stakes. SlateSims' edge is the ownership model — the sharpest, most-tested number in the space — wired into a full sim and contest-ROI stack.
