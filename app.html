Run the sim, build, then tweak Min/Max and rebuild until your exposures look right.

Lineup Pool

Estimates each lineup's expected ROI against an ownership-weighted field and a modeled payout structure, scored across your simulated tournaments. Build a pool first (tab 2). Payouts are modeled approximations for ranking lineups by contest-fit — not exact DraftKings prize tables.

Export

Download a DraftKings upload file, or fill your reserved entries template — each entry gets a unique best lineup (ranked by contest ROI if you ran a Contest Sim, else Sim Score), in DraftKings' Name (ID) format. Run the DK sync first so names map to upload IDs.
